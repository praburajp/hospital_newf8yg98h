About Cardiology

HMS Heart Centre is an important unit of Trust-run HMS Hospital which is fuelled by the motive to serve all sections of the society. HMS Heart Centre is one of the best heart institutes in the country providing cardiac care for over 25 years.

HMS Heart Centre was established by the vision of our Chairman Dr. Bakthavathsalam. He is the backbone and instrumental for the all-round development of the institute. He has infused the medical professionals and employees with HMS Culture of Quality, Teamwork, Service Motto, Moral Integrity, Compassion towards the patients, Commitment to work and Innovation.

HMS Heart Centre incorporates ethical practices and the latest technological advancements in patient management.

HMS Heart Centre offers a whole range of cardiology and cardiothoracic surgical services from early disease detection to complex interventions. Our diagnostic services include ECG, 2-D and 3-D Echocardiography, Stress Echocardiography, Transesophageal Echocardiography and Treadmill Testing.
